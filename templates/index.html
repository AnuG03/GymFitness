Tips to maintain -

  • Stay Active Daily:

Engage in regular physical activity, mixing strength training, cardio, and flexibility exercises to keep your body strong and functional.

  • Fuel Your Body with Whole Foods:

Eat a balanced diet full of nutrient-dense foods—fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and healthy fats—to support energy and muscle recovery.

  • Prioritize Recovery:

Allow your body time to recover by getting enough sleep and taking rest days to prevent burnout and injury.

  • Stay Consistent and Set Goals:

Consistency is key! Set achievable fitness goals and track your progress to stay motivated and committed.
